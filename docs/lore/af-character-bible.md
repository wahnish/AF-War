Adult Fantasy started as parody. It got complicated.
Inspired by 1990s American comics and the anarchic wit of Venture Brothers, these characters were originally conceived for laughs. They still are — but the jokes land harder now, the stakes feel real, and Hyper Brooklyn has developed a gravity of its own.
Twenty-five characters. One very strange universe. No one gets out unscathed.

## Description

Sergeant Tricera-Cop is the real deal, big cheese, in his own mind. He has the power of Dino Might. He's the macho in "macho men." A glorified mall cop, Tricera-Cop's beat is a bit of a mystery. He's more easily encountered in local watering holes than in more traditional settings for men in uniform. He dreams of being transferred to the Robot Repair Mall in Gravesend. He will definitely pass the Raze exam "next time." Tricera-Cop never takes bribes, but how much are you offering?

## Description

After many failed military exploits, General Hurricane Steele was about to retire. On his last day of service a mysterious incident occurred. Transmogrified, he begins investigating odd supernatural occurrences in Brooklyn. And, he needs help. Due to a lack of funds, he's forced to hire from the bottom of the barrel. Under his leadership, a squad of low-powered, rag-tag hooligans "volunteer" for what at first appearances is the cakewalk job of the century. Together, they unwittingly enter the epicenter of evil in the galaxy.

## Description

As a young boy, Mage Wolf was rescued from a tragic accident in which his parents were killed. He was nursed back to health and then raised by his grandmother, a shaman, and his grandfather, a wizard. In his feverish nightmares and healing dreams, he discovered an afinity for wolves. When he came of age, his grandparents sent him into the wilds to train in the ancestral traditions. He formally adopted the wolf as his totem animal and came into his unique magical powers.

---

> **Did You Know:** Mage Wolf is able to use transmutation magic to transform himself and others into various creatures and forms. He denies it but habitually goes missing during nights with a full moon.
